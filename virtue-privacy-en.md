# Virtue Privacy Policy

[**查看中文版本**](virtue-privacy-zh.html)

1. **Introduction**
   Virtue ("the App") is operated by **CrossZan**. This policy describes how we handle your data. The short version: we collect nothing. Everything stays on your device.

2. **Data We Collect**
   - The App does **not** transmit, upload, or sync any data to us or any third party.
   - All data you enter — habit records, virtue check-ins, daily reflections, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults).

3. **How We Use Data**
   - Your data is used solely to render the App's features on your device.
   - We never use it for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any secondary purpose.

4. **Data Storage and Security**
   - **Local only**: all App data resides on your device; we have no remote access to it.
   - **No account required**: the App works entirely without registration or sign-in.
   - **Access control**: we recommend enabling a device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to prevent unauthorized access to your records.

5. **Third-Party Sharing**
   - The App does not share your data with any third party.
   - The App contains no third-party SDKs, advertising libraries, or analytics frameworks.

6. **Children's Privacy**
   - The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
   - If you believe a child has entered data without permission, you can delete it directly within the App or by uninstalling the App.

7. **Your Rights**
   - **Access**: view all your records at any time within the App.
   - **Delete**: clear individual entries or reset all data from the Settings screen.
   - **Full removal**: uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.

8. **Updates to This Policy**
   - We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change. Updates will be noted in the App or on this page. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

9. **Contact Us**
   - Email: **dev@crosszan.com**
